Kai Chatbot Privacy Policy

At Kai, we prioritize the privacy and security of our users. By using Kai, you agree to the following privacy policy:

  • We do not sell the data or conversations with Kai to third parties.
  • Kai stores messages to improve user experience. Please refrain from sharing sensitive information that you do not want stored.
  • No one has access to the messages or conversations stored by Kai.
  • Kai does not share or leak conversations.
  • Kai may process images for certain functionalities but does not save them.
  • Kai may process voice messages for certain functionalities but does not store them.
  • Kai learns from conversations to improve its performance and provide better assistance.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy policy, please contact us.
